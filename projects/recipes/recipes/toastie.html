Toastie Recipe

A simple yet delightful toastie you can take on the go or when in a rush.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices bread
    • <1i>1 tbsp butter
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 2 slices of salami

Method:

  1. Butter one side of both bread slices.
  2. Place the first bread slice on sandwhich press and layer with cheese and salami as required. Finally layer the last bread slice, ensuring the butter is on the outside.
  3. Close the sandwhich press and toast for the desired amount, serve warm.
